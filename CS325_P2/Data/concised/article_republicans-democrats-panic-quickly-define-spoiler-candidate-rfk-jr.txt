The Battle for the Middle - Inside the Fight to Define Robert Kennedy Jr. as an Independent Presidential Candidate for the 2024 Election"

This article delves into the behind-the-scenes efforts of both Democrats and Republicans to shape the public perception of Robert Kennedy Jr. as he considers an independent run for president in 2024. With his iconic name and unpredictable views, Kennedy presents a unique challenge for both parties as they strategize and try to sway voters away from supporting him. As election day approaches, the stakes and tensions continue to rise.
