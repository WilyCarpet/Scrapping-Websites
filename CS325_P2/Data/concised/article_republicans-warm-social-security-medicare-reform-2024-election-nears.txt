The Looming Battle of Entitlement Reform: How Republicans Plan to Tackle the Rising National Debt and Save Social Security and Medicare"
This article examines the growing concern among Republicans about the rising national debt and the need for entitlement reform. It explores discussions within the party about raising the Social Security eligibility age and making cuts to Medicare, as well as recent proposals from Republican lawmakers. It also delves into the potential impact of the November election on this issue and how former President Trump's stance on entitlement reform could play a role. Interviews with GOP strategists and politicians provide insight into the challenges and potential solutions for addressing the nation's growing debt and protecting programs like Social Security and Medicare.
